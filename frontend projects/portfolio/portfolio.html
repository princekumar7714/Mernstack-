A **frontend developer** is a professional who builds the visual and interactive parts of a website or web application that users interact with directly. They use technologies like **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript** to create responsive, user-friendly interfaces. Frontend developers work closely with designers to bring layouts and wireframes to life and may also collaborate with backend developers to integrate APIs and data. Their goal is to ensure a seamless and engaging user experience across different devices and browsers.
